Our Story

Where Sound Becomes Legacy

At RIM Records, we sculpt sound into timeless art. Based in Nairobi, we fuse authentic Kenyan rhythms with world-class production to help artists create music that travels far beyond borders.

Whether it's reggae roots, soulful RnB, high-energy Gengetone or smooth rhumba — every project is treated with passion, precision, and purpose.
