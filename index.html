Our Story

Where Sound Becomes Legacy

At RIM Records, we don't just record music — we sculpt sound. Based in Nairobi, we blend traditional Kenyan energy with modern production techniques to help artists tell their stories and reach the world.

From reggae and rhumba to RnB, Gengetone, and beyond — every session is crafted with passion, precision, and purpose.
